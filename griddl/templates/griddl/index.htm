What is Hyperdeck?

Hyperdeck is a notebook programming environment for analysis and communication. It's a medium-weight and easy-to-use platform for tasks usually done with standard office software - analyzing data, running code, and writing documents.

Hyperdeck has been designed from the ground up to live on the web - the interface is a lot like writing a webpage in a webpage. The modern browser environment is powerful, fast, extensible, ubiquitous - and entirely different than the world of the 80's that your office suite was designed for. It's time to move forward.
